Features to Implement. 

Input
- Upload a Markdown / plain text file or fill a structured form
- Edit content directly in the app (not just upload and done)

Resume Section
- Personal info (name, title, contact, links)
- Work Experience
- Education
- Skills
- Optionally: Projects, Certifications, Languages, Volunteering

Output

- Export to PDF — clean, print-ready
- What you see should closely match what you get (WYSIWYG-ish)

Templates

- At least 2–3 professional templates to choose from
- Each template should feel distinct (not just color swaps)

Customization

- Change accent color
- Switch font family
- Control spacing/density (compact vs spacious)
- Reorder sections (drag and drop ideally)


Nice-to-Have (But Meaningful)

- Live preview as you edit — this is what makes FlowCV feel good
- Save progress in browser (localStorage) so work isn't lost on refresh
- ATS-friendly mode — simple single column, no icons or graphics
- Hide/show individual sections
- Multiple pages handled gracefully


Extra features

- Unlimited PDF exports
- More than 1–2 templates
- Custom colors and fonts
